Investigation Overview¶

The overall goals of this presentation is show the relationship between a variety of features that are present in our loan dataset, such as BorrowerAPR, BorrowerRate, ProsperRating (Alpha) and much more.

Dataset Overview¶

This is a Loan dataset provided by Prosper, it contains wide variety of features, both quantitative and qualitative. The dataset contains 113,066 rows and 81 columns, which are the features. The important features here are BorrowerAPR, BorrowerRate, LoanStatus, ProsperRating (Alpha) and Term length of the loan.
